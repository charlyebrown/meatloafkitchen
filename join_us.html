Join Us For A Meal

When: Every Saturday

Breakfast – 9:30 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. Join us for pastries, coffee and fruit.

Lunch – 1:00 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. Join us for our delicious meatloaf lunch, served with salad, side dish, fresh fruit and dessert! Vegetarians welcome.
